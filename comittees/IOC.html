The International Olympic Committee (IOC) is the governing body responsible for organizing the Olympic Games and promoting the Olympic values worldwide. Founded in 1894, the IOC oversees the planning and execution of both the Summer and Winter Games, ensuring they align with the principles of sport, excellence, and fair competition. It also works to promote the role of sport in society and supports the development of athletics globally through various programs and initiatives.
