Who is with us:

  • General Assembly

    The General Assembly (GA) is the main deliberative body of the United Nations. It provides a forum for debate on global issues and serves as a platform for all member states to come together and discuss challenges ranging from political conflicts, environmental issues, economic disparity, and much more. GA delegates work to craft resolutions that promote international cooperation and peace.

  • Security Council

    The Security Council (SC) is responsible for maintaining international peace and security. It handles conflicts between nations, peacekeeping operations, and the prevention of violent acts of aggression. With five permanent members and ten elected members, the SC works to address and resolve issues ranging from armed conflicts, sanctions, and humanitarian crises to the disarmament of weapons of mass destruction.

  • Human Rights Council

    The Human Rights Council (HRC) addresses global human rights violations, advocating for the protection of fundamental freedoms and social justice for all individuals worldwide. This committee works to promote the adherence to international human rights standards, respond to abuses, and engage in dialogues that aim to improve the human rights situation in countries and regions in crisis.

  • Economic and Social Council

    The Economic and Social Council (ECOSOC) plays a critical role in the United Nations’ efforts to promote sustainable development. It addresses issues such as poverty, social equity, climate change, education, and economic growth. The ECOSOC fosters collaboration between international organizations and civil society to create policies that ensure inclusive development and tackle the challenges facing marginalized communities around the world.

  • World Health Organization

    The World Health Organization (WHO) is at the forefront of global health policy. It works to improve public health through the prevention of disease, the promotion of healthy living, and response to health emergencies. The WHO’s work spans infectious diseases, mental health, maternal health, and health system strengthening, providing expertise and leadership to safeguard the health and well-being of populations worldwide.
