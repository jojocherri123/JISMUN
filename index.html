About JISMUN

The JIS Model United Nations (MUN) conference provides an opportunity for students to experience international diplomacy and engage in global issues. Students collaborate and debate to find solutions for pressing worldwide problems, gaining skills in negotiation, public speaking, and teamwork. Delegates represent countries in various committees, from the General Assembly to specialized bodies, and work to resolve challenges through constructive dialogue and negotiation.
